# Characters

The artist

A well known carpenter that is known for their wood carving. often carves cultural motifs into the beams of the houses he helps build

The Healer

A retired Hunter with a missing arm. Taken up healing and dressing so that fewer hunters end up like her

Celebrity

The Top Hunter of the Town, a loud and large man with a big axe and big beard. Speaks his mind but, when the cards are down is a good person.

Sage

The town elder, an old woman with tattoos a crooked back and much wisdom for thoes seeking it
